About Khmer Bridge

Khmer Bridge is a simple, free platform that connects Khmer language learners with native speakers and teachers around the world.

How it works

  1. Post your ad - Whether you're looking to learn, teach, or exchange languages
  2. Browse listings - Find people who match what you're looking for
  3. Connect directly - Contact each other using the provided information
  4. Meet and learn - Arrange lessons, exchanges, or conversations

What we offer

  • Free listings - No fees to post or browse
  • Direct contact - Connect via email, WhatsApp, Telegram, etc.
  • Global reach - Find connections anywhere in the world
  • All levels - From complete beginners to advanced speakers
  • Flexible arrangements - Free exchanges, paid lessons, or casual conversation

Safety first

We encourage all users to:

  • Meet new contacts via video call first
  • Use secure payment methods for paid services
  • Trust your instincts about people and situations
  • Report any problems to our team

Community guidelines

To keep our community welcoming:

  • Be respectful and professional in all interactions
  • Provide accurate information in your listings
  • Honor your commitments and be reliable
  • No spam, inappropriate content, or fake listings
